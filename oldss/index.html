Welcome!

This site is under construction. Go to this one.

I am a Professor in the Department of Economics at Federal University of Pelotas. I work with applied econometrics, more precisely with time series analysis, finance and recursive macroeconomics. Here you will find a lot of course materials (mostly in portuguese), R scripts, LaTeX templates, emacs configuration files and other interesting things.

If you are a student, you probably want to take a look at my teaching materials and the FAQ. If you really need to talk to me (i.e there is something not answered in the FAQ), then you can send me an email, but I might not answer right away, so the best way to meet me would be to make me a visit at Tuesdays from 4 to 6 pm at Room L in the Department of Economics of UFPel (Campus Porto - 4ยบ floor).

If you are a good student, you probably want to take a look at my my softwares section and my resources section too, and maybe read my posts below. If you are not there yet, but want to be a good student, then you should read my student tips.

If you are a researcher or professor, you might want to take a look at my posts, my softwares section, or my github repository. If you think it would be great to work with me in some project, please email me.

If you are a professional looking for some consulting or lecture, take a look at my portfolio and my short CV. If you want me to quit my job and accept your job offer, there is a good chance i'll do it if it is outside of Brazil and Latin America. I am currently searching for postdoctoral fellowships also (specially in Australia and the USA).
