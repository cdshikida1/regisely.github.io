Welcome!

This site is under construction. Go to this one.

I am a Professor in the Department of Economics at Federal University of Pelotas. I work with applied econometrics, more precisely with time series analysis, finance, banking and recursive macroeconomics. Here you will find a lot of course materials (mostly in portuguese), R scripts, LaTeX templates, emacs configuration files and other interesting things.

If you are a student, you probably want to take a look at my teaching section and the FAQ. If you need/want to talk to me (i.e there is something not answered in the FAQ or you have a bright idea and want my help), then you can send me an email or pay me a visit at Tuesdays from 4 to 6 pm at Room L in the Department of Economics at UFPel (Campus Porto - 4ยบ floor). If you are a good student, you probably want to take a look at my softwares section and my resources section too, and maybe read some of my posts below.

If you are a researcher or a professor, you might want to take a look at my posts below, my softwares section or my github repository. If you think it would be great to work with me in some project, please email me.

If you are a professional looking for some consulting or lecture, take a look at my portfolio and my short CV.
